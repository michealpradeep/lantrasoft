NOCC Services

Our NOCC services (Network Operations Command Center) are not just restricted to network monitoring and management. With our experience and expertise in the IT Infrastructure and Application Management Services spaces, we offer customized and extended NOCC services to meet customer requirements in several other areas including mission critical applications monitoring, security patching and environment monitoring services.

The NOCC offers 24x7 coverage with aggressive Turnaround Times (TAT) and SLAs by a team of highly skilled Network Operations professionals.

NOCC Services

Our NOCC services address the following Key Performance Areas (KPA) in the Customer environments.

  • Network Monitering And Management
    • Network Latencies
    • Throughput and Performance
    • CPU load on the Network Servers
    • Firewall Rules Monitoring
    • Circuit Break/Down Issues
    • Voice Gateway Circuit Issues
    • VOIP/Soft Phone Service Errors
  • Servers Monitoring And Management
    • Windows and Linux Servers Monitoring
    • Servers Maintenancz
    • Patch Management for Servers
  • Applications Monitoring
    • Up-time and Availability Monitoring
    • Real-time Application Performance Monitoring
    • Ecommerce Site Monitoring
    • Database Performance Monitoring
    • Batch Job Applications Monitoring
  • Faults And Events Management
    • Incident Management
    • Problem Management
    • Escalation Path Management
  • Security Events Monitoring
    • Phishing Email Alerts - Filtering with Spam definitions
    • Brute Force Access pattern/behaviour detection
    • Detection of Malware Objects - Infection-match and Web-infection detection
