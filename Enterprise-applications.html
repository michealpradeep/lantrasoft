Enterprise Applications

Enterprise Applications are crucial to business continuity. We understand that the mere availability of the Enterprise Applications is critical to businesses and has a direct impact on the productivity of the whole Company. At the same time, we help in taking this day-to-day support overhead completely off your list.

Lantrasoft offers end-to-end Enterprise Applications Support that addresses the problem of your productive hours spent on daily IT support issues and enables you to focus on core business initiatives.

  • Active Directory Administration
    • Multi-domain single forest and Multi-forest Enterprise Network
    • DNS, DHCP, DFS Administration and Performance Tuning
    • Automation of AD Objects creation
    • Fine grained configuration of AD Policies
    • Snapshot, AD Recycle Bin and Backup/Restore functions
  • Group Policies
    • Security Groups and elevated functions
    • Creation of Service Accounts
    • Interactive and Non-interactive modes
  • Server Administration
    • Physical (HP, CISCO, DELL) and Virtual Servers Management
    • Server Build, Security Hardening, Patching and Remediation
    • Operating System Troubleshooting and Performance Tuning
    • Asset Inventory Management
    • Capacity Management
    • IIS, FTP, SFTP, RAID and Cluster Administration
    • Effective Management of File System and Share permissions
    • Maximum up-time and Application availability
  • OS and Security Patch Management
    • Evaluation and selection of Enterprise OS Security and Critical Updates
    • Creation of Software Update groups and Deployment packages
    • Creation of Device/Machine Collections for grouping and patching
    • Inventory Management of the Host Machines
    • SCCM, Spacewalk and Satellite administration for Patch Management
  • Office 365 Administration
    • Creation and administration of Mailboxes and Objects
    • Execution and enforcement of O365 policies using PowerShell scripts
    • PowerShell scripts based License Management
    • Message Tracing
    • Support for e-mail delivery issues
